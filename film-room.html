Questions

Film Room FAQ

How should we film the game?

Elevated and wide beats close and shaky. A phone on a tripod at midfield, filming from a few rows up if possible, following the ball loosely so I can see the players around it. If your club already uses Veo or Trace, that footage is perfect.

Full match or highlights?

Full match, always. Highlights show what went well; development lives in everything else — positioning off the ball, reactions after mistakes, the runs that were never rewarded. That's what my breakdown is for.

How long does it take?

Your written breakdown arrives within 5 days of submission. During peak season (tryouts, playoffs) I'll tell you the current turnaround before you commit.

Who sees my video?

Only me. Your links stay private — I never share or post them — and you can use an unlisted link that only works for people who have it.

Does this work if I train with another club or coach?

Yes — the Film Room is fully club-neutral and remote. Many players use it exactly this way: their club runs team training, and my breakdown gives them an individual development lens their team environment can't.
