Inside the Lab

The game between the lines

The halfspaces are the channels between the wings and the center — the zones where the best players in the world receive, turn, and break games open. Playing there takes more than a clean first touch. It takes awareness before the ball arrives.

That's the standard in the Lab. I sharpen technique the way games actually demand it: under pressure, with a decision attached to every touch. And I use film to show players what they can't feel in the moment. The result is a player who doesn't just work harder — they see the game earlier.
